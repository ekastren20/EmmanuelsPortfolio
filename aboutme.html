Welcome
to my portfolio, and thank you for your interest. Me? Me... I am a creative, yet analytical brain. My education and background involves STEM, as I started my collegial journey at Purdue studying engineering. However, there is a side to me that thirsts for creativity. The "Renaissance Man" of the family, as my mom calls me. Which is perhaps why I doodled in text books, created a Pokemon style card game, play guitar and create oil paintings. In the end of my collegial journey, I finished in a more "Liberal Arts" kind of education, environmental science. I pursued interesting jobs, one involving plenty of recreational outdoor activity.

Something else caught my attention though. UX/UI. While I have dabbled in graphic design using Adobe Illustrator, I never realized the expansiveness of a computer's creation technology. In June of 2022 I earned a certificate in UCF's UX/UI Design Bootcamp where I learned about the extensive research involved in user experience and the technical and collaborative practice of user interface. Since then I have been practicing by building with coding languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript and C#. Though I enjoy artistic creation, when it comes to computer design there's nothing like building from the ground up. This is what coding allows me to do. In September of 2023, I landed an IT position with Mid Atlantic Finance, my first ever job related to computer technology. While working this position, I have been close to the company's excellent developers, learning about their work and how I can potentially contribute. I am currently working on a UI project for an inhouse business application. It's been an interesting career shift, but I'm excited to unfold whatever designer-self I will become in this tech-influenced society.
