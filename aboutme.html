Hello There!

Welcome to my portfolio, and thank you for your interest. I suppose what's most important about me, and also what has brought you here (because it's always better to have a symbiotic relationship), is that I am a creative, yet analytical brain. My education and background involves STEM, as I started my collegial journey at Purdue studying engineering. However, there is a side to me that thirsts for creativity. Which is perhaps why I finished in a more "Liberal Arts" kind of education. I finished with environmental science. I pursued interesting jobs, one involving plenty of recreational outdoor activity.

Something else caught my attention though. UX/UI. After dabbling with Adobe Illustrator I never realized the expansiveness of computer development skills. In June of 2022 I earned a certificate in UCF's UX/UI Design Bootcamp and have been practicing while learning new development skills along the way. It's been an interesting career shift, but I'm excited to unfold whatever designer-self I will become in this tech-influenced society.
