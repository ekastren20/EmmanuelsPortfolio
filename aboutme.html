About


In 2022, I took a career shift from environmental science to tech design and development. That year, I learned a compacted UX/UI education under UCF's UX/UI Design Bootcamp Program.

Since October of 2023, I've had a role as an IT Specialist and a UI developer working in front-end coding to build the interface and functionality of various projects. This position requires me to utilize knowledge in HTML, CSS and JS while understanding C#, .Net, Blazor and Bootstrap. I've also created HTML forms while helping to implement Hubspot, a marketing tracker, into pages on our main site. But, what I am enjoying most is taking the lead UI developer role for our Blazor, C# Application Prototype, which will replace our current web-based application. This is an improvement project with a large scope and is in early development. You can look at this current project here.
